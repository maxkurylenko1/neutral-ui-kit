# Foundations

Our design system tokens and patterns.

## Colors

HSL-based color system with automatic dark mode support.

**Light Theme:**

- Background: white
- Foreground: dark gray
- Primary: dark blue-gray
- Secondary: light blue-gray

**Dark Theme:**

- Background: dark blue-gray
- Foreground: near white
- Colors adjust automatically

## Typography

- Font: Inter
- Scale: 12px to 24px

## Spacing

Tailwind default scale (0.25rem increments)

## Border Radius

- sm: 4px
- md: 6px
- lg: 8px
- xl: 12px
